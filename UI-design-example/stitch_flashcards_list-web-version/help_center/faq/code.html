Top Questions

How do I reset my password?
expand_more

You can reset your password from the login screen by tapping "Forgot Password." We'll send a reset link to your registered email address.

How does the progress tracker work?
expand_more

Our progress tracker automatically logs your study time and completed lessons. You can view your daily, weekly, and monthly stats in the 'Progress' tab.

Can I use StudyFlow offline?
expand_more

Yes, you can download lessons and materials for offline access. Your progress will sync automatically once you're back online.
